THIS IS HOW YOU BENEFIT FROM THE POWER OF THE COMPOUND INTEREST EFFECT WITH THE TOP INVESTMENTS.

Did you know that with an interest rate of 8% and an investment period of 10 years, your capital more than doubles? After ten years, an investment sum of 10,000 euros, for example, becomes an impressive 21,589 euros. This corresponds to an increase in assets of over 115%. If you instead only receive interest of 2% for the 10,000 euros over the same period, you will only receive 12,190 euros at the end of the term. A wealth increase of only 12%. If you take inflation and taxes into account, your assets will even decrease with an interest rate of less than 4%! And what about after 30 years, for example? While 10,000 euros at 2% have not even doubled to 18,114 euros, you will receive an impressive 100,627 euros with an interest rate of 8%.
